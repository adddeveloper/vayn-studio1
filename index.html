Donnie Bowen

Hi, my name is Donnie Bowen. Some may say I was predestined to become a real estate agent. Son of real estate icon, Don Bowen, I was taught the ins and outs of the business from a very young age. Being able to experience first hand the functioning of the California real estate market for the last 37 years has prepared me to be a conscientious and caring real estate agent. I feel blessed to have this opportunity to help support, guide and serve great families with their real estate needs. Growing up in Coto de Caza in the late 1980s and early 1990s holds memories I will cherish forever. Fishing in the ponds, skeet shooting with my father at the old hunt club, and just living the Coto motto of, “Life never looked so good.” Now a three-time Ironman finisher, loving husband to Tali Bowen, and proud father of London and Isabella Bowen, I find myself even more inspired to serve our community by raising money for cancer research, orphaned children in Africa, and disabled triathletes. I feel the greatest lesson I have learned from being a Father is that life is not all about me, but about what I can also do for others. Since our father’s passing in November of 2012, I have felt a great responsibility to carry out the same quality of care and devotion for each of our clients. As my father always taught me, the client is number one. I look forward to our family helping your family with all of your real estate needs.

An additional room currently used as an office and features a bar could be converted to a 3rd bedroom. It also features a full bath and was conveniently placed there for easy access to clean off after tending to the grounds or horses. The spacious Primary Suite features an enclosed patio that is currently used as an arts and crafts room. The en-suite features a double vanity and walk-in shower. Down the hall is an expansive secondary bedroom and full bathroom. The wrap-around yard is beautifully landscaped and provides a great space for enjoying nature. Connected to the expansive driveway is the road leading to the various equestrian facilities, the casita and an additional 6-car garage. All easily accessible by walking or driving, you will be amazed by the surreal landscape surrounding this incredible equestrian estate. Don't miss this rare opportunity to own this beautiful piece of rustic paradise on over 13 acres of usable, flat land. Additional equestrian approved site plans are also available for the possibility of having a total of 80 horse stalls, two arenas, two walkers, and two round pens.
